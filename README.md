<h2>How to run</h2>
<ol>
    <li>npm install</li>
    <li>npm start</li>
    <li>open http://localhost:3000/?</li>
    <li>Enter a food item into the search field and hit submit</li>
    <li>Enjoy browsering through the different recipes! Are you hungry yet?</li>
</ol>
